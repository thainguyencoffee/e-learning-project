When there are no students enrolled in a course, you can proceed with unpublishing it without facing significant risks. However, if there are students who have purchased the course but have not yet completed the payment, unpublishing the course will greatly impact their purchasing experience in the future. In this case, you will be responsible for compensating any issues that arise as a result. On the other hand, if you decide to unpublish a course that already has enrolled students, you will need to provide compensation to those students if you take this action.
